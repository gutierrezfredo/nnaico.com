# Product

## Overview

**What:** AI platform for creating specialized business assistants with custom knowledge bases and integrations
**For:** US-based companies, 50-500 employees

## Value Proposition

"Your company's AI brain" - combines LLM intelligence with company data for instant answers, support, training, and business insights.

## Core Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Knowledge Base | Train on docs, SOPs, FAQs - instant answers for staff and customers |
| CRM Integration | Connect to sales data - ask questions about leads, deals, performance |
| Business Insights | "What's selling best?" "Which rep is behind target?" - data in plain English |
| Training | New hires ask questions instead of interrupting senior staff |
| Customer Support | 24/7 answers for common customer questions |

## Target Customers

**Primary:** US-based companies, 50-500 employees, with high training needs

### Target Industries

| Industry | Why they need it |
|----------|------------------|
| SaaS | Fast growth, support overload, scattered docs |
| Hospitality | High turnover, constant training, multi-location |
| Healthcare/Clinics | Staff training, patient FAQs, compliance |
| Retail | Product knowledge, policies, seasonal staff |
| Manufacturing | SOPs, safety procedures, compliance |
| Construction/Trades | Project procedures, safety, certifications |
| Franchises | Consistency across locations, franchisee training |

**Common threads:**
- High turnover + lots of procedures = constant training burden
- Data in CRMs/systems but no easy way to query it = missed insights

**Target Contacts:** Head of Ops, Head of Sales, Head of HR, Head of CS, CEO/Owner

### Pain Points

| Pain Point | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| New hires take weeks to be productive | Training assistant answers questions instantly |
| Senior staff waste hours re-explaining | AI handles repetitive questions 24/7 |
| Knowledge leaves when employees leave | Captured in AI, never lost |
| Scattered docs nobody can find | One AI brain that knows everything |
| Support ticket overload | Customer-facing assistant for common questions |
| Can't easily query CRM/sales data | Ask in plain English: "Show me deals closing this month" |
| No visibility into team performance | "Which rep is behind target?" - instant answer |
| Don't know what's selling | "What are our top 5 products this quarter?" |

## Key Differentiators

- Trained on YOUR company data (not generic like ChatGPT)
- Multiple specialized assistants per company
- Turnkey setup ($150-200/mo vs $50k+ custom dev)

## Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Assistant | AI agent with custom knowledge, tools, and behavior |
| Knowledge Base | Company documents, FAQs, procedures fed to assistant |
