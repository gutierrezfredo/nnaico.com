# Product

## Overview

**What:** AI platform for creating specialized business assistants with custom knowledge bases
**For:** Small-medium businesses (10-100 employees)

## Value Proposition

"Your company's AI brain" - combines LLM intelligence with company data for instant answers, support, training, and insights.

## Target Customers

| Industry | Pain Point | Solution |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| E-commerce/Retail | Repetitive customer questions | Support assistant trained on products/policies |
| Professional Services | Slow new hire onboarding | Training assistant with procedures |
| SaaS Companies | Support ticket overload | Customer support + internal knowledge |
| Agencies | Team knowledge silos | Centralized assistant for processes |
| Course Creators | Students ask same questions | Post-purchase support assistant |

## Key Differentiators

- Trained on YOUR company data (not generic like ChatGPT)
- Multiple specialized assistants per company
- Turnkey setup ($150-200/mo vs $50k+ custom dev)

## Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Assistant | AI agent with custom knowledge, tools, and behavior |
| Knowledge Base | Company documents, FAQs, procedures fed to assistant |
